## The Core Principle

Treat domain choice as one input to the naming decision, not proof that a name is viable. Domain prices,
TLD conventions, registrar policies, and handle availability change; verify them at decision time.

---

## TLD Strategy

**Evaluate:**
1. The exact `.com` when it matters to the audience or category
2. Relevant established or category-specific TLDs, considering cost, renewal terms, trust, and confusion risk:
   - Tech/AI: `.io`, `.ai`, `.dev`, `.cloud`
   - Creative: `.studio`, `.art`
   - Finance: `.vc`, `.capital`, `.fund`
3. Geographic TLDs such as `.nyc`, `.ca`, `.us`, or `.fr` when location is part of the brand

---

## Modifier Strategies

When the .com isn't available, these feel natural in most categories:

**Action-oriented prefixes:**
- `getname.com`, `tryname.com`, `joinname.com`, `helloname.com`, `usename.com`

**Descriptive suffixes:**
- `namelabs.com`, `namehq.com`, `nameapp.com`, `nameteam.com`

**Collective framing:**
- `wearename.com`, `teamname.com`, `madebyname.com`

**Word variants:**
- Drop vowels, tweak spelling, try an abbreviation
- Repeat the word: `namename.com` (surprisingly often available and memorable)

**Domain hacks:**
- Split the word across the TLD: e.g., `deli.cious`, `bit.ly`
- Use rare TLDs for clever endings: `.one`, `.run`, `.world`, `.global`

## Social Handle Strategy

- **B2B/SaaS/ecommerce** â€” Brand memorability matters more than exact handle match. Content > handle precision (especially on TikTok, where successful brands run hundreds of handles).
- **Fashion, art, beauty, lifestyle** â€” A perfect Instagram/TikTok handle carries outsized weight. Worth prioritizing.
- **General rule** â€” Identify the channels that matter, then verify availability and impersonation risk on each current platform before acting.

## Acquiring Premium Domains

- Prices, marketplaces, and broker practices change. Record the registrar or marketplace, quoted price,
  renewal terms, and date checked before comparing acquisition options.
- Treat a premium domain as a tradeoff against audience, trust, budget, and launch needsâ€”not an automatic
  requirement or automatic deferral.
